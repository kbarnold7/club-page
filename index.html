Fantasy Book Club

Who are we?

The Fantasy Book Club is a safe space for anyone and everyone that has a love for fantasy novels. It's important to use to include all kinds of stories in our monthly rotations. Wether you enjoy the classics, New Adult or Romantasy, there will a book for everone!

How do I join?

We are an open door group that can accomodate anyone that wants to join. We meet every Wednesday at 7pm in Conference Room 10 at the Ellison Library. Simply show up and join the conversation or just listen if that's your vibe. There will never be pressure to contribute!
